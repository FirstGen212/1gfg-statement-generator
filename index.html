1GFG Employee Deduction Statement Generator

Upload a census file, get one branded PDF statement per company plus a data-review report. Everything runs in your browser — no file ever leaves this page.

How it rolls up

  • Virtual = every row's SIMERP price, summed
  • In-Person = FGP Amount for rows tagged MEC or MEC+, summed
  • Wholelife = Eligible Policy amount, summed
  • Companies with "test" in the name are skipped entirely
  • Rows missing SIMERP or FGP are held back and listed in a Needs Review PDF (a missing Employee ID alone does not hold a row back)
